10. MantraLock (Android App)

This section describes data practices specific to MantraLock, our Android app that helps you stay focused by locking chosen apps until you recite a mantra aloud. MantraLock has no user accounts and no backend server. Except for the purchase handling described below, everything MantraLock does happens on your device.

Accessibility Service

MantraLock uses Android's Accessibility Service for a single purpose: to detect which app is currently in the foreground, so that when you open an app you have chosen to lock, MantraLock can show its recitation screen. The Accessibility Service does not read, collect, log, or transmit the content of your screen, text you type, or your activity inside other apps. This detection happens entirely on your device and no information from it ever leaves your device.

Microphone and speech recognition

When you recite a mantra to unlock, MantraLock uses your device's microphone and your device's built-in speech-recognition service to count and verify your repetitions. MantraLock does not record, save, or transmit your audio. Depending on your device and settings, the speech recognition itself may be performed on-device or by your system speech provider (for example, Google); that processing is governed by that provider's own privacy policy. MantraLock never receives or stores your voice recordings.

Installed apps

So you can choose which apps to lock, MantraLock reads the list of apps installed on your device to display them in a picker. This list is used only on your device and is never transmitted to us or anyone else.

Data stored on your device

Your settings, selected mantras, locked-app choices, streaks, and recitation history are stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or have any access to this information. Uninstalling MantraLock deletes it.

Purchases

MantraLock offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock full functionality. Payment is processed by Google Play. We use RevenueCat to confirm and restore your purchase; for this, a pseudonymous app-user identifier and basic device information are sent to RevenueCat over an encrypted connection. This is used only to verify your purchase status and does not include your name, email, or the on-device data listed above. See RevenueCat's privacy policy for details of their handling.

11. Family Habit Tracker (Mobile App)

This section describes data practices specific to Family Habit Tracker (Android package com.crazylittleventures.FamilyHabitTracker), our app for helping families build and track habits together. Family Habit Tracker has no user accounts and no backend server. Except for the purchase handling described below, everything the app does happens on your device.

Data stored on your device

The habits, family member profiles, rewards, streaks, and settings you create are stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or have any access to this information. Uninstalling the app deletes it, and you can also clear it at any time from within the app's settings.

Photos

You may choose photos from your device's photo library or camera to use as family icons, profile pictures, or theme backgrounds. These images are stored only on your device and are never uploaded to us or any third party.

Backups

The app lets you export your data to a backup file so you can save or move it between devices. If you choose to share that file (for example, via email or Google Drive), that action is entirely under your control; we do not receive or have access to it.

Notifications

With your permission, the app schedules local reminders on your device to help you keep up with habits. These notifications are generated on your device and involve no server or transmission of personal data.

Purchases

Family Habit Tracker offers an optional one-time "Premium Lifetime" in-app purchase. Payment is processed by Google Play. We use RevenueCat to confirm and restore your purchase; for this, a pseudonymous app-user identifier and basic device information are sent to RevenueCat over an encrypted connection. This is used only to verify your purchase status and does not include your name, email, or the on-device data listed above. See RevenueCat's privacy policy for details of their handling.

No analytics or advertising

Family Habit Tracker does not use analytics SDKs, advertising, or tracking, and does not collect your name, email, or usage data.

Children

Family Habit Tracker is designed for families to use together and is intended to be set up and managed by an adult (13 or older). It collects no personal information online and transmits no data about any family member, including children, to us or any third party.
